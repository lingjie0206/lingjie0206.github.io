About Me.

I am a Lise Meitner Postdoctoral Research Fellow working with Prof. Christian Theobalt in the GVV group at the Max-Planck-Institute for Informatics. I received my Ph.D. degree at the University of Hong Kong in 2019, advised by Prof. Wenping Wang. Before starting my PhD, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2014, supervised by Prof. Hai Jin. My research interests include neural rendering, human performance capture, and 3D reconstruction.
